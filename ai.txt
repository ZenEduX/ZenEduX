## PRODUCT DESCRIPTION
ZenEduX is a K12 AI-powered education platform designed specifically for Chinese students from Grade 1 to Grade 12, with a focus on high school (Grades 10-12). Unlike generic AI chatbots that simply provide answers, ZenEduX employs Socratic guided learning to help students develop genuine understanding and independent thinking skills. The platform features an AI teacher persona named Xiao Chan Laoshi (小禅老师), who combines the academic depth of Peking University (北大) with the proven teaching wisdom of Huanggang methodology (黄冈).
